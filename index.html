Biographies

       I am a Ph.D. candidate in Mreal Lab @ NTU from August 2021, advised by Prof. Hanwang Zhang. I am also served as a research scholar in ASTAR, xxx, advised by Prof. xxx. Before that, I received my Bachelor's degree from Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Ji Wu and Prof. Yirong Wu.

       I am currently focusing on the topic of NLP interpretability, with interests in large language models, causal inference, and reasoning. Specifically, I am intrigued by the research question of endowing LLMs with reasoning abilities by mimicing human thinking processes. I’m graduating around Jul. 2025 and open to discussions on employment opportunities in Singapore.
